World Class Library

Library is a heart of any educational institute. The central library is established with stack room and issue section as well as with reading room with sufficient area as per the AICTE norms. The university has sufficient numbers of titles and volumes of textbooks and reference books in addition with magazines, national and international journals. It is also strengthened with e-books, e-journals and digital library. The university has a 5000 sq.ft Central Library of its own where constant attempt is made to upgrade the reading list in each subject along with infrastructure. The completely automated open-source library houses around 5,000 titles and 10,000 volumes that come.
